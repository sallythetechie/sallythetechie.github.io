Places I have visited in Ghana

Coco Vanilla

From the name to the ambiance..speaks to you of the fun to expect. After one week of design thinking, Friday night found us at this cool place for Karaoke night. Armed with a few drinks and a free spirit my friends and I sang and danced to the music.

Madina Market

Some say it is the largest market in Accra is known for unbeatable prices and varieties of kente. Either for bulk or singular purchase, one can browse the busy labyrinths where loads of shops and mildly haggling hawkers sell the colourful yarns. I had the opportunity to board a Trotro and head there in search for veggies and true to the Ghanains words, the market did not disappoint
